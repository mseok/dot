# Report Checklist

Run this checklist before finalizing a draft.

## Evidence

- Is every strong claim supported by the provided material?
- Does the draft distinguish achieved implementation from planned expansion?
- Are benchmark claims framed within their actual evaluation conditions?

## Section Fit

- Does the section do what the writing guide asks, not just summarize the topic?
- Are paragraph roles visible and non-overlapping?
- Does the section cover the required axes such as development, utilization,
  validation, or expansion when the form requires them?

## Tone

- Does the prose read like an official Korean report?
- Are blog-style transitions removed?
- Are repetitive AI rhythms removed?
- Are technical terms explained once, then used consistently?

## Evaluation Friendliness

- Can a reviewer quickly see what was built, how it was used, how it was
  checked, and why it matters?
- Does the prose make the evaluation criteria legible without sounding like a
  rubric dump?

## Final Pass

- Remove mirrored sentence openings.
- Compress redundant summary sentences.
- Replace vague praise with a named mechanism or concrete implication.
